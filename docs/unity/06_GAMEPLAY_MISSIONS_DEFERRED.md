# Gameplay Missions Deferred

## Current Scope

Open-world missions are not part of the active milestone.

Do not implement:

- LiteraQuest mission scenes;
- PE/Health mission scenes;
- Science mission scenes;
- 90-mission catalog;
- mission objectives;
- mission progress;
- mission rewards;
- world restoration.

## Future Direction

Future missions will be fixed/static game content by grade, subject, and term.

Possible future full target:

```text
3 subjects × 3 terms × 5 missions × 2 grades = 90 missions
```

This target is not active until approved.
