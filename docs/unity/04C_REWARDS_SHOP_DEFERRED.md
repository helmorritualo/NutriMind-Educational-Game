# Rewards, Inventory, and Item Shop Deferred

## Current Decision

Rewards and item shop are deferred until gameplay missions are finalized.

Do not implement:

- spendable coins;
- EXP economy;
- item shop;
- inventory;
- cosmetics;
- pets;
- titles;
- equipment;
- purchases;
- mission reward grants;
- world unlock rewards.

## Allowed Now

Quiz result screen may show:

- score;
- pass/fail;
- feedback;
- display-only star/badge text if needed.

These must not create spendable currency or inventory.

## Future Plan

After mission content is finalized:

```text
Mission completion → coins/EXP/badge/unlock → item shop/inventory/cosmetics
```

The economy must be balanced after the mission reward rates are known.
